WORLD GOAL CAMPAIGN

PROJECT-DESCRIPTION

For the project ‘World Goal Campaign’, we had to choose in groups a company that had an accessible style guide / design manual. For this, we had to choose one of the 17 world goals that we wanted to work with. The task was to design a landing page for a world goal campaign for the chosen company, as well as make a video for the page. In this project we had to work with project management, design brief, visual identity, style guide and video.

To see landingpage

FEEDBACK

- Good prototype-work in Adobe XD

- The location of the video needs to be more prominent as it is an important touchpoint

- Only have one earth in the logo, as it gives a wrong image in relation to the fact that we only have one earth that we have to take care of.

MODIFICATION AFTER THE FEEDBACK
