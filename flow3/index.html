BUILD AN APP

PROJECT-DESCRIPTION

For the project ‘Build an app’ I had to design and create a prototype of a mobile app. I had to use AdobeXD to demonstrate the screens and their flow, as well as use Adobe Illustrator for graphics for the interface and Adobe Photoshop for images. First I had to choose a company or an organisation and imagine that they will go to market with an app where there is interaction with the UN’s world goals. The app had to be developed for both mobile and tablet and there had to be content in at least 3 menu items and at least 3 levels. In addition, suggestions for interface elements were to be developed, including icons for the app, all drawn in Adobe Illustrator.
